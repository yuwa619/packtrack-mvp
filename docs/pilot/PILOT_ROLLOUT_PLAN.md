# Pilot Rollout Plan (30 Days)

## Cohort Plan
- Cohort 1: 3-5 SMEs (Week 1-2)
- Cohort 2: 10-20 SMEs (Week 3-4)
- Scale decision after day 30 based on pilot metrics and support load

## Entry Criteria
- Expected invoice volume: at least 20 documents in the first 2 weeks
- Named point of contact: one operational owner and one backup
- Willingness to review: participant can triage review tasks weekly
- Data readiness: invoices include supplier, date, line details, packaging hints, and weights where possible

## Week-by-Week Schedule
- Week 1: onboarding, access setup, first uploads, baseline timing capture
- Week 2: Cohort 1 active processing, weekly check-in, issue triage and configuration fixes
- Week 3: Cohort 2 onboarding and activation, continue Cohort 1 operations
- Week 4: full pilot run, quality and turnaround review, rollout recommendation

## Success Metrics
- Time-to-report: median elapsed time from upload finalise to report generated
- Review-task rate: percentage of processed documents that create review tasks
- Extraction coverage: percentage of required fields captured across processed documents
- User satisfaction: weekly qualitative scorecard (confidence, usability, and net value)

## Risks and Mitigations
- Poor scan quality:
  - Mitigation: ingestion guidance, minimum scan quality checklist, re-upload flow
- Missing weight or unit:
  - Mitigation: reviewer correction workflow and targeted follow-up with supplier teams
- Taxonomy ambiguity:
  - Mitigation: review queue for ambiguous classifications and weekly taxonomy decision log
- Pilot support overload:
  - Mitigation: staged cohort expansion and fixed weekly triage cadence
